Purpose, Audience, and Goals

Purpose

The Purpose of the website is to provide a central location for information for individuals with type one diabetes.

Audience

The site is designed to target common users who are type 1 diabetic women considering pregnancy, are pregnant, or have recently given birth, as well as their families.

Goal

The goal of this website is to increase the reach of an established social media presence.



Persona

Name

Scarlett B. Jayne

Age

30

Technology

Most of Scarlett's time online is spent on her iPhone 11 with unlimited 4G data running the latest operating system.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Scarlett is a frequent user of social media who follows a few different type 1 diabetic influencers and listens to pregnancy podcasts. She is an occasional user of websites like WebMD.com and BeyondType1.com for medical information, but she needs more specific information and resources she can trust in once place.

Frustrations and Needs

Scarlett is frustrated with the lack of resources available to her and women like her. She has had type 1 diabetes for 25 years. While she has access to medical care, she has received conflicting information about type 1 diabetes and pregnancy. She would like to start a family, but she is scared about the risks. While she has tried to find information on this topic online, most websites are geared toward gestational diabetes.

Goals

Scarlett hopes to gain knowledge about pregnancy and type 1 diabetes that will allow her to be confident in her decisions about her future.

User Story

As a casual user, I want to see accurate, fact based information about pregnancy with type 1 diabetes presented in an easy-to-follow manner.
